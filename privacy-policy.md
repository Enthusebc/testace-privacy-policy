Privacy policy

Effective date: 15 January 2026

TestAce (“the app”) is developed and published by Enthuse Business Consultancy (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect user information when you use the TestAce mobile application, available on the Google Play Store. 
We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring compliance with Google Play Developer Policies and applicable data protection principles.

**1. Information we collect**

TestAce may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal information
- Name or username (if provided during registration)
- Email address (used for authentication, communication, and account recovery)
- Grade level and selected subjects (for learning personalisation)

1.2 Usage and technical information
- App interaction data (quiz attempts, scores, progress tracking)
- Device information (device model, operating system, app version)
- Log data (timestamps, error logs, crash reports)

1.3 User-generated content
- Comments, posts, and replies in discussion forums
- Uploaded images or learning materials (where applicable)

**2. How we use your information**

We use collected information to:
- Provide and personalise learning content and quizzes
- Track academic progress and performance analytics
- Enable community discussions and interactions
- Improve app functionality, performance, and stability
- Send important notifications related to app features or updates
- Manage subscriptions and premium features
- Ensure security, prevent abuse, and enforce app policies

**3. Third-party services**

TestAce uses trusted third-party services that may collect information to provide their services:

3.1 Google Admob
- Used to display banner, interstitial, or rewarded advertisements.

AdMob may collect:
- Advertising ID
- Device and usage data

Ad serving may be personalised or non-personalised depending on user settings and age-related requirements.

3.2 Firebase services
Firebase is used for:
- Analytics (Firebase Analytics)
- Crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)
- Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

These services help us understand app usage and improve reliability.

3.3 Supabase
Supabase is used for:
- User authentication
- Secure data storage
- Real-time database features

User data stored in Supabase is protected through access controls and security rules.

3.4 Google play billing
- Used to process subscriptions and in-app purchases.
- We do not collect or store payment card details. All billing information is handled securely by Google.

**4. Children’s privacy**

TestAce is designed for students, including learners under the age of 18.

- We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal data from children.
- Data collected is strictly limited to educational and functional purposes.
- Advertising is configured to comply with child-directed and family-friendly content policies where applicable.
- Parents or guardians may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of a child’s information.

**5. Data sharing and disclosure**

We do not sell or rent personal data.
Information may be shared only:
