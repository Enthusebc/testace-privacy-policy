# TestAce – Account Deletion

**Developer:** Enthuse Business Consultancy  
**App name:** TestAce

## How to request account deletion

Users can request deletion of their TestAce account and associated personal data using one of the following methods:

### Option 1: In-app request
1. Open the TestAce app
2. Go to **Profile**
3. Select **Settings**
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm the deletion request

### Option 2: Email request
If you are unable to access the app, send an email from your registered email address to:

**enthusebusinessconsultancy@enthusebc.com**

Use the subject line:  
**TestAce – Account Deletion Request**

---

## What data is deleted

When an account deletion request is completed, the following data is permanently deleted:

- User profile information
- Email address
- Quiz results and progress records
- Posts, comments, and discussion activity
- Uploaded images or attachments
- Device and session identifiers linked to the account

---

## Data that may be retained

The following data may be retained for a limited period:

- Anonymous and aggregated analytics data (non-identifiable)
- Records required for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes

---

## Deletion timeline

- Account deletion is processed within **30 days**
- Once deleted, the account **cannot be recovered**
